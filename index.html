SPINOZAS DOG

Experimental Rock • Psychedelic Folk • Noise

Spinozas Dog is a sonic collective formed in 2022. We create music that lives between philosophy and chaos — raw, emotional, and untamed.

Inspired by Spinoza’s quest for truth and the wild freedom of stray dogs, we blend jagged guitars, hypnotic rhythms, tender melodies, and noise into something uniquely ours.
