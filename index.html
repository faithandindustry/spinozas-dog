Peter Sant • Kristian Craig • Mark Axiak

Spinozas Dog are an experimental instrumental trio based on the island of Malta. They create textural ambient, post rock via free improvisation.

Featuring the original guitar stylings of film maker Peter Sant (Żafżifa). Treating the instrument as sound device using adapted capos, irregular tunings & metallic chop sticks, but not afraid to keep it simple, melodic and classic at the same time.

On Bass Mark Axiak: a prolific player who’s traversed stages and studios from Australia, New York, Berlin & Oslo. Taking the four string plank into synthesiser realms, with pitched reverse loops and filters.

Kristian Craig aka producer Capitol K. Once described as ‘the punk warlord of groove’ for his electronic programming plays full drum kit, He's inspired by trad rythum, jazz n blues shuffles, using open tone and light touch.
